About Us

Fournos is a three-generation, family-owned restaurant in San Leandro, California. Curated with nostalgic recipes from the old country, our flavors come straight from the ovens of Greece and Italy. The heart of our restaurant’s success is rooted in culture, family, and tradition. Our hope is that our food makes you feel like part of our family.

Fournos Favorites

  • Saganaki

    Flambéed Greek Kasseri cheese—opa!

  • Greek Salad

    Crisp, colorful, and full of Greek flavor. Romaine lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, green bell peppers, Kalamata olives, feta cheese

  • Blue Caesar

    A bold twist on Caesar with blue cheese and bacon. Whole-leaf romaine lettuce, house-made blue cheese dressing, Danish blue cheese crumbles, chopped bacon, cherry tomatoes

  • Margherita Pizza

    Fresh and classic—just like it should be. San Marzano tomato sauce, Galbani fresh mozzarella, fresh basil

  • Spaghetti Puttanesca

    Savory, briny, and full of character. Anchovies, Kalamata olives, capers, house-made marinara sauce

  • Gyro Pita

    The Greek wrap that does it all. Lamb and beef, tomatoes, red onions, tzatziki sauce, fries, pita bread

  • Meatball Stromboli

    Cheesy, saucy, stuffed, and golden. Grande cheese, pork and beef meatballs, garlic, house-made pizza sauce

  • Tiramisu

    Our famous tiramisu—creamy and indulgent. Espresso-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cream, cocoa powder
