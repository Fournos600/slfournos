About Us

Fournos is a three-generation, family-owned restaurant in San Leandro, California. Curated with nostalgic recipes from the old country, our flavors come straight from the ovens of Greece and Italy. The heart of our restaurant’s success is rooted in culture, family, and tradition. Our hope is that our food makes you feel like part of our family.

Accessibility & Privacy Statement

At Fournos, we are committed to making our website accessible and welcoming to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We are actively working to improve the user experience and aim to follow applicable accessibility standards such as the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG).

If you experience any difficulty accessing our website—such as viewing our menu or navigating any page—please let us know:

  • Which page or feature you were trying to access
  • A brief description of the issue
  • The device, browser, or assistive technology you were using (if applicable)

We will make every reasonable effort to address your concern promptly. If needed, we are happy to read our menu to you over the phone, describe any item in detail, and take your order verbally.

Data & Privacy Practices (California Consumer Privacy Act – CCPA Disclosure)

We respect your privacy. Our website does not collect personal information such as names, emails, or phone numbers unless you contact us directly.

However, like most websites, basic technical data (such as browser type, device information, and general usage patterns) may be collected automatically through cookies or analytics tools to help us improve performance.

  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Our website may include links to third-party platforms (such as Instagram, Google Maps, Yelp, or Facebook). These sites operate under their own privacy policies, which we do not control.
  • If you wish to limit cookies or tracking, you may adjust your browser settings at any time.

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to request access to, deletion of, or information about any personal data we may hold. Since we collect minimal information, requests can be handled quickly upon contact.
